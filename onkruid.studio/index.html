Onkruid believes public space is the reflection of society and its collective culture. We want our projects to redevelop that public space to positively reshape the cities & world we live in. We collaborate with companies, organizations and cities through empathy, creativity and sound judgement. We specialize in designing identity, creating momentum & building experiences.

Onkruid is an interdisciplinary team combining expertise in architecture, public policy, marketing, music & culture to further develop the creative context of our cities. Onkruid develops actions, interventions or events that unite people & cultivates parts of the social wasteland.
